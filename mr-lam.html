Mr. Lam

Field: Product Support

How computer science is used in their field:

As a product support technologist, computer science is primarily used in his field to provide technical support for products. This includes debugging, testing and troubleshooting hardware, financial, usage and cost analysis, and accessing the vast amount of documentation for the numerous products they support. Computers are also used for frequent communication between management and coworkers.

How computer science could be used in their field:

Computer science, specifically artificial intelligence, could be used in the future to automate most tasks.

How computer science has been used in their field:

Previously, testing and financial analysis took a long time to complete due to limited hardware, such as RAM and CPU speeds. The lack of software also meant that they had to manually troubleshoot and perform calculations, which could take many days. Documents consisting of many pages also had to be printed out, which could also take a few days.

Computer science now allows them to very quickly and efficiently support products and also save the environment.
